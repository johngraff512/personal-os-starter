# Building a Second Brain

*(Example faithful extraction. In a real item this holds the clean markdown of the
article body — what the `/kb` pipeline produces from a URL drop or Web Clipper capture.
Replace with your own content.)*

A second brain is just a place to put the things you don't want to lose. The trick is
not the capturing — it's making the captured things easy to find and connect later.
Plain text files, organized lightly and indexed once a day, do this better than any
single app, because they survive the app.
